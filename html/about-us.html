About us

Abuyee.

Abuyee is a proud franchise and trusted partner of Eurotherm, an Omani brand renowned for its commitment to quality plumbing solutions. With manufacturing rooted in Raysut Industrial City, Sultanate of Oman, Eurotherm has set a high standard in producing advanced PPR pipes and fittings engineered for durability and performance.

At Abuyee, we bring this legacy of excellence to our customers, delivering Eurotherm's high-performance products backed by reliable service and deep market understanding. Every pipe and fitting we offer is crafted to meet the most demanding applications—ensuring long-term reliability, safety, and peace of mind.

Our mission is to make world-class plumbing solutions accessible across markets by building a strong network of dealers and distributors, just as envisioned by Eurotherm. With a shared dedication to quality—both in product and after-sales support—we aim to be a trusted name for professionals and industries alike.
