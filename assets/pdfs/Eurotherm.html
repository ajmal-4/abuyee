About Eurotherm

“Eurotherm” is an Omani brand for PPR pipes and fittings; started with the aim of providing quality plumbing solutions. Manufacturing plant is located in Raysut Industrial City, Sultanate of Oman. Quality is engineered into Eurotherm products during the entire manufacturing process. The Company is with policy to serve its customer with quality; both in products and after sale services and with vision to reach far and wide of world market by developing dealers and distributors for the brand.
